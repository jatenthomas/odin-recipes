Cheesecake

Ingredients

  • Crust:
    • Graham cracker crumbs (or digestive biscuits)
    • Sugar
    • Melted butter
  • Filling:
    • Cream cheese (softened)
    • Sugar
    • Eggs
    • Vanilla extract
    • Sour cream or heavy cream (optional, for creaminess)
  • Optional toppings:
    • Fresh fruit (strawberries, blueberries, etc.)
    • Fruit compote or jam
    • Chocolate or caramel drizzle

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Mix graham cracker crumbs, sugar, and melted butter in a bowl.
  3. Press the mixture firmly into the bottom of a springform pan to form the crust.
  4. In a large bowl, beat the cream cheese until smooth.
  5. Add sugar and mix until combined.
  6. Add eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition.
  7. Stir in vanilla extract and sour cream or heavy cream (if using).
  8. Pour the filling over the prepared crust and smooth the top.
  9. Bake for 50â€“60 minutes, or until the center is set but slightly jiggly.
  10. Turn off the oven and let the cheesecake cool inside for about an hour.
  11. Refrigerate for at least 4 hours, or overnight, before removing from the pan.
  12. Add optional toppings before serving.
