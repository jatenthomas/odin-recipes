Cookies

Description

These cookies are soft, chewy, and packed with chocolate chips. Perfect for a quick snack or dessert, they have a slightly crisp edge and a gooey center that everyone will love.

Ingredients

  • Dry Ingredients:
    • All-purpose flour
    • Baking soda
    • Salt
  • Wet Ingredients:
    • Unsalted butter (softened)
    • Sugar
    • Brown sugar
    • Eggs
    • Vanilla extract
  • Optional add-ins:
    • Chocolate chips or chunks
    • Chopped nuts (walnuts, pecans, etc.)
    • Dried fruit (raisins, cranberries)

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. In a medium bowl, whisk together the flour, baking soda, and salt.
  3. In a large bowl, cream together the softened butter, sugar, and brown sugar until light and fluffy.
  4. Add the eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition, then stir in vanilla extract.
  5. Gradually mix in the dry ingredients until just combined.
  6. Fold in chocolate chips, nuts, or dried fruit if using.
  7. Drop spoonfuls of dough onto the prepared baking sheet, spacing them about 2 inches apart.
  8. Bake for 10â€“12 minutes, or until the edges are golden but the centers are still soft.
  9. Allow the cookies to cool on the baking sheet for a few minutes, then transfer to a wire rack to cool completely.
  10. Serve and enjoy!
