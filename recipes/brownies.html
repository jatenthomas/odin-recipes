Brownies

Description

These brownies are rich, fudgy, and packed with chocolate flavor — the perfect treat for any chocolate lover. With a chewy center, slightly crisp edges, and that signature shiny, crackly top, they strike the perfect balance between gooey and firm. Whether enjoyed warm with a scoop of ice cream or packed for a quick snack, brownies are a timeless dessert that’s simple to make yet always impressive.

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • Sugar
  • Eggs
  • All-Purpose flour
  • Cocoa Powder
  • Salt
  • Vanilla extract

Optional but common add-ins

  • Chocolate Chips
  • Nuts
  • Espresso Powder

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Grease a baking pan or line with parchment paper.
  3. Melt the butter in a small saucepan over low heat or in the microwave. Let it cool slightly
  4. Mix the sugar and melted butter in a large bowl until combined
  5. Add the eggs, one at a time, beating well after each addition
  6. Stir in the vanilla extract
  7. Sift together the flour, Cocoa powder, and salt, then gradually fold them into the wet mixture until just combined.
  8. Optional: Fold in chocolate chips, nuts, or espresso powder for extra flavor
  9. Pour the batter into the prepared baking pan and spread evenly.
  10. Bake for 25-30 Minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out with a few moist crumbs.
  11. Cool the brownies in the pan on a wire rack before cutting.
  12. Serve and enjoy!
